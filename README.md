# 🌿 Sahaaya Setu – Inclusive AI Scheme Eligibility Helper for India

## Overview

Sahaaya Setu is an inclusive, multilingual web platform designed to help Indian citizens discover government welfare schemes they may be eligible for. The platform simplifies the process of finding relevant schemes by collecting a few user details and matching them with curated government programs using deterministic eligibility logic.

The project aims to bridge the gap between citizens and social welfare opportunities by making government information easier to understand, more accessible, and available in multiple Indian languages.

---

## Problem Statement

Many people in India remain unaware of government schemes intended to support them. Existing information is often scattered across multiple websites, written in complex language, or inaccessible to individuals with limited digital literacy.

As a result:

* Eligible beneficiaries miss out on important welfare programs.
* Citizens struggle to understand complicated eligibility criteria.
* Language barriers prevent wider access.
* People rely on unofficial sources that may provide inaccurate information.

---

## Solution

Sahaaya Setu provides a simple and inclusive interface where users can:

* Enter basic demographic and socioeconomic information.
* Receive likely government scheme matches with eligibility scores.
* View document checklists required for applications.
* Access verified official government links directly.
* Ask an AI-powered guide questions about schemes, documents, and application processes.
* Use the platform in multiple Indian languages.

## How It Works

1. Users provide a few personal and socioeconomic details.
2. The system compares these details against curated scheme criteria.
3. Relevant schemes are ranked based on eligibility scores.
4. Users receive:

   * Scheme recommendations
   * Document checklists
   * Guidance from the AI assistant
   * Official links for further action

## Disclaimer

Sahaaya Setu is an informational support platform. Eligibility results generated by the system are indicative and should not be considered official decisions.

Users are encouraged to verify all information through the linked government portals before proceeding with applications.
